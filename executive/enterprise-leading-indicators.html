Enterprise Leading Indicators Documentation

Overview

This enterprise-grade dashboard monitors 56 leading indicators across 14 operational domains for a 50,000 agent global contact center operation. These indicators are specifically designed to:

  • Predict customer experience outcomes before surveys are collected
  • Identify cost drivers and revenue risks in real-time
  • Enable proactive capacity management across 12 global regions
  • Optimize multi-vendor technology stack performance
  • Monitor compliance with PCI, TCPA, GDPR, and CCPA regulations
  • Support real-time decision making for operations leadership

Domain Structure (14 Domains, 56 Indicators)

Domain Focus Area # Business Impact

Network & Connectivity Latency, site connectivity, bandwidth 4 Voice quality, agent productivity
Voice Quality & Telephony MOS scores, queue times, trunk capacity 4 Customer experience, NPS

Agent Desktop & VDI Citrix, DEX scores, VDA health 4 Agent efficiency, AHT

Operational Efficiency Shrinkage, AHT, occupancy, schedule 4 Labor cost, capacity
